Group Fitness

Our Classes

Eight world-class group fitness programs led by certified instructors. Every level welcome — from first-timer to seasoned athlete.

What We Offer

Find Your Fit

Whether you want to build strength, burn calories, find your zen, or just have fun — we have a class for that. All classes are included with your membership.

Strength 🏋️

Body Pump

A high-rep barbell workout that targets all major muscle groups. Designed to tone, strengthen, and build lean muscle — your classic go-to for a full-body burn.

Cardio + Strength 🔥

HIIT

High-Intensity Interval Training that alternates intense bursts of exercise with short recovery periods. Maximum calorie burn, maximum results — in minimum time.

Full Body ⚡

Boot Camp

Military-inspired functional training that combines strength, cardio, and agility drills. Push your limits and discover what your body is truly capable of.

Combat Fitness 🥊

Boxing

Learn the fundamentals of boxing in a safe, supportive group setting. Build explosive power, sharpen coordination, and get an incredible full-body workout in the ring.

Combat Cardio 🦵

Kickboxing

Combines punches, kicks, and cardio intervals for an energizing total-body workout. Burn up to 800 calories while learning strikes and combos that actually make you feel powerful.

Flexibility + Mind 🧘

Vinyasa Yoga

A flowing sequence of postures linked with breath. Builds flexibility, balance, and mental clarity. The perfect complement to your strength training and the best way to unwind.

Cardio 🚴

Spin Cross

An intense indoor cycling experience that blends sprint intervals with climbs and off-the-saddle movements. A low-impact, high-energy ride that will leave you dripping and energized.

Classic Cardio 🪜

Step Training

The iconic choreographed step workout that never goes out of style. Improve cardiovascular endurance and coordination while having a blast — all levels absolutely welcome.
