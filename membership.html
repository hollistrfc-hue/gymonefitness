Membership Perks

What's Always Included

These aren't upsells — they're part of being a Gym One member.

🔑

24/7 Keycard Access

Your membership comes with a keycard so you can access the gym any time of day or night — 365 days a year. Train on your schedule, not ours.

👶

Free On-Site Daycare

Bring your kids to the gym without guilt or extra cost. Our free daycare service means parents can focus fully on their workout while the little ones are safe and happy.

🏋️

Group Classes Included

No per-class fees, no booking headaches on Standard and above. Just show up for Body Pump, HIIT, Yoga, Boxing, and more — whenever the class fits your schedule.

🚿

Full Facility Access

Showers, lockers, free parking, air conditioning, and a well-maintained facility. Everything you need before, during, and after your workout is right here.

Personal Training Available

Ready to accelerate your results? Our certified personal trainers can create a custom program tailored to your goals. Ask us about current PT rates and availability.

No Long-Term Contracts

We believe you should stay because you love it, not because you're locked in. Flexible month-to-month memberships are available — no commitment required to get started.
