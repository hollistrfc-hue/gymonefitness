Gym One Fitness has been the heartbeat of Stillwater's fitness community since 2005. We're not a corporate chain — we're your neighbors, your coaches, and your biggest supporters.

Stillwater's Gym Since 2005

For over 20 years, Gym One has been the gym Stillwater comes back to. We know your name, we know your goals, and we're genuinely invested in your success. This is what a real community gym looks like — and there's nothing else like it in town.

Modern Equipment, Spotless Facility

Owners Daniel and Sarah Bassett have made serious investments in top-tier equipment and facility upkeep. You'll never wait for machines or settle for outdated gear. Gym One delivers a premium experience at a locally competitive price.

Every Level, Every Goal

Whether you're walking into a gym for the first time or chasing your next personal record, Gym One is built for you. Our certified personal trainers and diverse class lineup support every fitness level — no intimidation, no pressure, just results.

Open 24 Hours a Day

Real life doesn't follow a 9-to-5 schedule — and neither does Gym One. Your membership includes round-the-clock keycard access so you can train before the sun comes up or long after it goes down. Your gym, your schedule, no exceptions.

Free Childcare — Included

Gym One is one of the only fitness centers in Stillwater offering complimentary on-site childcare with every qualifying membership. Drop off the kids with our caring staff, step into the gym, and get a focused, guilt-free workout every single time.

Proud to Be Stillwater's Own

Joining Gym One means more than a monthly membership — it means joining a gym that's been part of this city's fabric for over two decades. Locally owned, deeply rooted, and committed to Stillwater for the long haul. Come see why the community keeps coming back.
