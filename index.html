Why Gym One?

More Than a Gym.
It's a Community.

We're not a corporate chain and we never will be. Gym One Fitness is Stillwater's home for real people with real fitness goals — no judgment, no pressure, just results.

🏡

A True Home Base

From your first visit you'll feel it — Gym One isn't just a building, it's a community where members and staff know you by name. We've been building that culture since 2005.

⚡

Top-Tier Equipment

Current owners Daniel and Sarah Bassett have invested heavily in modern equipment updates. Expect a clean, well-maintained facility that keeps pace with the best gyms anywhere.

🎯

Every Level Welcome

Whether it's your first week or your tenth year, Gym One meets you where you are. Our certified personal trainers and diverse class lineup support every fitness level and goal.

🌙

Open When You Are

Life doesn't run 9-to-5. With 24/7 keycard access, you can train on your schedule — early morning, late night, or any time in between. No excuses, no restrictions.

👶

Free Childcare Included

Parents deserve to crush their workouts too. Bring the kids — our free on-site daycare means zero guilt and zero logistics. Just show up and train.

📍

Right Here in Stillwater

We're locally owned, locally operated, and deeply invested in the Stillwater community. When you join Gym One, you're supporting a business that cares about this town.
