About Gym One

Twenty years of community, growth, and genuine commitment to the people of Stillwater.

Where It All Began

Built on Community,
Rooted in Stillwater

Gym One Fitness first opened its doors in 2005, founded by Barry and Vanessa Patton with a simple but powerful belief: that a great gym should feel like home. In a market dominated by corporate chains and monthly auto-pay traps, Gym One was different — personal, welcoming, and genuinely focused on the people inside the building.

Over nearly two decades, that founding philosophy took root. Members didn't just come to work out — they came because they knew the staff, because they had friends in the weight room, because Gym One was a real part of their lives. That kind of loyalty isn't bought; it's built, one conversation and one workout at a time.

Today, Gym One Fitness is owned and operated by Daniel and Sarah Bassett, who are deeply invested in the Stillwater community and share the same vision that started it all. Under their ownership, the gym has seen meaningful equipment upgrades, facility improvements, and an expanded class lineup — all while preserving the intimate, community-first atmosphere that makes Gym One special.

We're not a franchise. We're not trying to be the biggest. We're just committed to being the best gym for the people of Stillwater, Oklahoma — every single day.
